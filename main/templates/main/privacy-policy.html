Welcome to Uniway!

We are thrilled to have you join our community of students, alumni, and educators. We are designed to foster a positive and supportive environment for all users. To ensure a safe and enriching experience for everyone, we have established the following community guidelines:

1. Privacy and Confidentiality: Protect the privacy and confidentiality of yourself and others. Do not share sensitive personal information, contact details, or any data that could compromise the security of individuals.

2. No Misuse of Information: Alumni details are provided to inculcate a learning environment through the use of resources available. Do not indulge in spam calls or messages to college alumni.

3. Reporting and Moderation: If you come across any content or behavior that violates these guidelines, please report it to the app administrators. Our moderators will review reports and take appropriate action.

4. Academic Integrity: Uphold academic integrity and honesty at all times. Do not engage in cheating, plagiarism, or academic misconduct.

5. Event and Initiative Promotion: You are welcome to share details about upcoming college events and initiatives, but ensure the information is accurate and appropriate. Any commercial promotion, unrelated advertisements, or spam is strictly prohibited.

6. Inclusive Community:Uniway values diversity and inclusivity. We do not tolerate discrimination based on race, ethnicity, gender, religion, sexual orientation, or any other personal characteristic.

7. Positive Environment: Foster a positive and supportive environment for learning and growth. Encourage others, celebrate achievements, and offer help to those who need it.

8. Abide by App Terms of Service: In addition to these guidelines, users are expected to adhere to the terms of service provided by the Uniway app.

Failure to comply with these community guidelines may result in actions such as content removal, temporary or permanent account suspension, or other measures deemed appropriate by the Uniway team.
Uniway is a platform for learning, networking, and personal growth. You are requested to be respectful and responsible members of this community.
